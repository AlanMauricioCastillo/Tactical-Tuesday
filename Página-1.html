Briefing

At the beginning of the operation, logistical maneuvers will be carried out, so that the FOB's are supplied, it is important to keep the troops at the front well supplied. Pilots will be assigned and convoy missions will be conducted for this purpose.

When crossing enemy lines will be assigned reconnaissance teams, this will allow us to have the advantage over the enemy to have full knowledge of the assets they have.

The field commander having the necessary information will decide whether to divide the forces, attack in combination, etc, etc.. Missions will be carried out until the 90 minutes from 7 pm EST or until the operations in process are completed.

At the end, the best team and the best team member will be chosen, not necessarily from the same winning team, and the players who heal the most and are first in the leaderboard will be recognized. And of course a photo of the operation to close.
